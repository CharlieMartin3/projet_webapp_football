About me

I am a young Data Engineer graduated from ESILV, passionate about football.
My journey has been shaped by my training experience at Paris Saint-Germain as a young player, from the age of 11 to 18.

With this dual experience, combining technical expertise and immersion in the world of high-level football,
I aim to make statistical analysis of this fascinating sport accessible.

Football, with its sometimes irrational nature, makes it a unique and magical sport.
Yet, behind this unpredictability lie data that help understand certain aspects and analyze performances.
